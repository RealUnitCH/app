## Form of Shares

The Series A shares of the company are structured as global certificates pursuant to Art. 973b CO and become book-entry securities within the meaning of the Swiss Federal Act on Book-Entry Securities dated 3 October 2008 ("FISA"). The main register is maintained at SIX SIS AG, Olten ("SIS").

The Series B shares of the company are structured as registered uncertificated securities in the form of tokens pursuant to Art. 973d CO. The tokenized shares qualify as Distributed Ledger Technology ("DLT") securities pursuant to Art. 2 lit. bbis of the Federal Act on Financial Market Infrastructures and Market Conduct in Securities and Derivatives Trading dated 19 June 2015 ("FinMIA").

## Dual Track

The Series A shares are admitted to trading and listed on BX Swiss. The Series B shares are not admitted to trading on BX Swiss and are not listed, but can be transferred as tokens via a blockchain (Ethereum).

The Series B shares have been made tradeable on the organized trading platform called Taurus Digital Exchange (TDX) operated by Taurus SA and Taurus (Europe) Ltd. In addition, the Series B shares have also been made tradeable on the honesto App operated by Honesto AG.
